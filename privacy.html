Data & Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Atlantic AI Digital Solutions

What is POPIA?

The Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) is South Africa's primary data privacy law, enacted to regulate how organisations collect, store, use, and share personal information. It gives individuals the right to know what data is held about them, how it is used, and the right to request its deletion.

Atlantic AI is fully committed to POPIA compliance. This policy explains in plain English what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

We believe privacy is a right, not a checkbox. We collect only what we need, store it securely, and never sell it to anyone.

What Data We Collect and Why

Depending on how you interact with Atlantic AI — as a website visitor, a lead, or a client — we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number, and business name submitted via our enquiry form or WhatsApp. Used to respond to your enquiry and manage our client relationship.
  • Conversation data — messages sent to AI agents we build and manage on behalf of clients. Used to train and improve the AI system as authorised by the client.
  • Usage data — anonymised analytics on how our websites and portals are used (page visits, session duration). Used to improve our products and services.
  • Business information — details you share about your business during the onboarding and strategy process. Used solely to build your digital solution.

We do not collect sensitive personal information (such as ID numbers, medical records, or financial account details) unless it is strictly required for a specific service and you have provided explicit consent.

How We Store and Protect Your Data

All personal data processed by Atlantic AI is stored on secure, encrypted infrastructure. We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data storage and transmission (TLS/SSL)
  • Access controls limiting data to authorised personnel only
  • Regular security reviews of our platforms and third-party integrations
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with geographic data residency controls

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by South African law. Client data is held for the duration of the contract and a reasonable period thereafter, unless deletion is requested.

Third-Party Services

Atlantic AI uses trusted third-party services to deliver our products. These may include cloud hosting providers, the WhatsApp Business API (Meta), and AI model providers. Each of these services has their own privacy and data processing agreements in place.

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Data shared with service providers is shared only to the extent necessary to deliver the service you have engaged us for.

Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject under POPIA, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access — you may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to correction — you may request that inaccurate or outdated information be corrected.
  • Right to deletion — you may request that your personal information be deleted, subject to any legal retention obligations.
  • Right to object — you may object to the processing of your personal information for direct marketing purposes.
  • Right to complain — you may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa if you believe your rights have been violated.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your personal information, or to exercise any of your rights under POPIA, please contact our designated Information Officer directly:

Email: reece@atlanticaiautomation.com
Subject line: Data Privacy Request

Please include your full name, the email address associated with your data, and a brief description of your request. We will respond within 10 business days.

If your request relates to data held within a client's AI system (i.e., conversations with a chatbot powered by Atlantic AI), please contact that client directly as they are the responsible party for that data under POPIA.
